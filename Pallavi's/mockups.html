Two AI mechanisms, one screen. Top panel — classification: the AI maps the email to one of 12 codified deal patterns (V1–V9 + N1 + G1 + D1) or flags it off-system. Mariana sees the match, the triggering phrases, and a dropdown to override. The 9 V-variants aren't bespoke chaos — they're a finite, regex-classifiable set (0 unmatched on 328 non-flat deals). Bottom panel — extraction + forced disambiguation: within the chosen template's schema, fields fill in. Recoups are a full axis, not a single field — one row per recoup line with category + cap + treatment. The Hwang-pattern prevention is the second flag: even when the agent's email mentions no other categories, the form forces a "confirm none allowed" answer. Save is blocked until all flags resolve. Prototype scope: only V1 fields are built. Other dropdown items would be greyed "schema not yet implemented" in the real product. AI parse is pre-filled synthetic data in this demo; live LLM is the next-ship.
Screen 5 · Disambiguation modal — the Coastal Spell case5 / 11
⚠ Resolve ambiguity · Recoup line 1 of 2 · Marketing
The email says:
"expense cap $3,200
marketing recoup not to exceed $500"
How is the $500 marketing recoup treated?
A · In the cap. Marketing is part of the $3,200 cap. Total recoupable = $3,200 (marketing included within it).
B · On top of the cap. Marketing of $500 is on top of the $3,200 cap. Total recoupable = $3,700.
▸ this is what Mariana picks
C · None of these — flag for variant review. Pushes to Mariana's taxonomy-extension queue. The slice treats Mariana as design partner for new patterns.
D · Other (free text). Logged for review; flag stays until reviewed.
Next flag after this: "Confirm no other recoup categories" (specialty backline, hospitality overage, production overage) — the Hwang-pattern prevention.
Confirm choice Cancel
Why this is the centerpiece: the canonical Coastal Spell dispute was exactly this question, 90 days late, $720 on the line. Forcing the choice at capture time prevents that loop. The second flag (next in the queue) is what prevents the Hwang pattern — 5 of his 6 hidden marketing-recoup pushbacks happened because nobody asked "did the deal cover this category in the first place?" Option C is the taxonomy-extension hatch the brief promised Mariana.
